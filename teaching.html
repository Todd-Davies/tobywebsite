Teaching

I studied at Oxford University and the Guildhall School of Music and Drama and hold a PGCE in Secondary Education. I have taught in a range of independent and state schools across the UK, most recently leading the music department in a large sixth form college in the north of England. I am also a GCSE Music examiner and have worked on education projects for the Barbican's Creative Learning division, the One Foundation and Oxfordshire County Music Service.


I regularly tutor piano and music theory (to grade 8), and composition and academic music (GCSE, A-Level and undergraduate degree level). I usually tutor online via Skype but can also travel. For more information or to arrange a lesson, please click here to get in touch!
